Dr. Jason Wang is a provincially licensed Doctor of Traditional Chinese Medicine and Acupuncturist who is also a long-time resident of Surrey for the last 20 years, serving patients in this growing city with dedication and care.

Dr.Wang also speaks Mandarin Chinese, Cantonese, Taiwanese Hokkien and Japanese.

The Doctor of Traditional Chinese Medicine(Dr.TCM) license is the highest licensure bestowed by the province of BC upon practitioners of Chinese Medicine and Acupuncture who has completed the 5-year Doctoral program of rigorous post-university professional training in the many paradigms of Eastern Medicine and successfully passed the national licensing exam.
